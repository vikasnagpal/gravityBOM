Use Cases

The game prototype can be extended in a number of ways. Some interesting use cases can be:

  • Induction programs

    The prototype can be extended to create induction programs for new joinees, replacing existing boring PowerPoint presentations and word documents. By adding gamification elements such as motivation, rewards and social recognition into the game, there could be a dramatic increase in the effectiveness of your induction programs.

  • Increase user engagement in websites

    Having a game version that runs on various browsers, encourages higher viewer engagement. User engagement on your company’s website can be increased to a great extent by embedding a version of the game which can be played by customers to explore your company rather than reading an “About Us” page, having a direct impact on the recall value of your brand.

  • 3D Business Models

    By using a 3D interactive version of your business model you give partners and investors a fresh and exciting way of viewing your company’s services.

  • Strategy modelling

    A similar approach can be taken towards strategy modelling. Instead of the boring charts and figures involved in creating a strategy, the prototype can be extended to facilitate modelling while the user plays a game. The use of game elements educates, guides and involves strategy managers in creating their business strategies in an innovative way. Additionally, the game can be made multi-player that can allow to co-design strategies collaboratively.
